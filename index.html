Placement Objectives:

  • To incorparate desision-making,problem solving and critical thinking skills in students.

  • To ignite the students’ Technical core knowledge and Soft skills to meet the corporate recruitment process.

  • To enhance overall performance in terms of personality development, career planning, goal setting and re-skilling which will stand them in good stead even after getting the job.

  • To Place the maximum number of students through campus interviews conducted by the top notch companies.

  • To Create awareness about “career planning” and “career mapping” among the students.

  • To Equip the students with life skills.

  • To Organize Various Training Programs to train the students in the areas of Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning and Verbal reasoning

  • Training the students through Mock Interviews to perform well in the professional interviews as per the expectations of the corporate world.
